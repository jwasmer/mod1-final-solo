Rock, Paper, Scissors

Choose your game!

Custom Mode allows you to build your own version of Rock, Paper, Scissors! Look through the fighters on the next screen and drag down your favorites.

There's only one rule--the number of fighters you choose has to be odd!

Now that you've picked your fighters, you get to decide who each fighter wins and loses to!

Here's how it works: Each fighter will LOSE against fighters to its left, and WIN against fighters to its right.

Move them around until you're satisfied, then click the button when you're ready to play!
